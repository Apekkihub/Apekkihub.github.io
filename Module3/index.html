top 3 Sci-fi movies

2001: Space Odyssey

Directed By: Stanley Kubrick
Released: 1968
IMDB score: 8.3
Cast: Keir Dullea, Gary Lockwood and William Sylvester
Synopsis: When a mysterious artifact is uncovered on the Moon, a spacecraft manned by two humans and one supercomputer is sent to Jupiter to find its origins.

The Thing

Directed By: John Carpenter
Released: 1982
IMDB score: 8.2
Cast: Kurt Russel, Wilford Brimley and Keith David
Synopsis: A research team in Antarctica is hunted by a shape-shifting alien that assumes the appearance of its victims.

Arrival

Directed By: Denis Villeneuve
Released: 2016
IMDB score: 7.9
Cast: Amy Adams, Jeremy Renner and Forest Whitaker
Synopsis: Linguist Louise Banks leads a team of investigators when gigantic spaceships touch down around the world. As nations teeter on the verge of global war, Banks and her crew must find a way to communicate with the extraterrestrial visitors.

top 3 horror movies

Suspiria

Directed By: Dario Argento
Released: 1977
IMDB score: 7.3
Cast: Jessica Harper, Stefania Casini and Flavio Bucci
Synopsis: An American newcomer to a prestigious German ballet academy comes to realize that the school is a front for something sinister amid a series of grisly murders.

Cabinet of Dr. Caligari

Directed By: Robert Wiene
Released: 1920
IMDB score: 8.0
Cast: Werner Krauss, Conrad Veidt and Friedrich Feher
Synopsis: Hypnotist Dr. Caligari uses a somnambulist, Cesare, to commit murders.

One Cut of the Dead

Directed By: Shin'ichirô Ueda
Released: 2017
IMDB score: 7.6
Cast: Takayuki Hamatsu, Yuzuki Akiyama and Harumi Shuhama
Synopsis: Things go badly for a hack director and film crew shooting a low budget zombie movie in an abandoned WWII Japanese facility, when they are attacked by real zombies.

Top 3 western movies

The Wild Bunch

Directed By: Sam Peckinbah
Released: 1969
IMDB score: 7.9
Cast: William Holden, Ernest Borgnine and Robert Ryan
Synopsis: An aging group of outlaws in 1913 Texas look for one last big score, selling stolen Army rifles to a rogue Mexican general during that country's revolution, as the traditional American West is disappearing around them.

Cemetery Without Crosses

Directed By: Robert Hossein
Released: 1969
IMDB score: 6.7
Cast: Robert Hossein, Michèle mercier and Guido Lollobrigida
Synopsis: A melancholic gunfighter is drawn into a vengeful and tragic kidnapping plot by his widowed ex-lover.

Once Upon a Time in the West

Directed By: Sergio Leone
Released: 1968
IMDB score: 8.5
Cast: Henry Fonda, Charles Bronson and Claudia Cardinale
Synopsis: A mysterious stranger with a harmonica joins forces with a notorious desperado to protect a beautiful widow from a ruthless assassin working for the railroad.
