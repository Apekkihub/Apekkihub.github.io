Top 3 Sci-fi Movies

2001: Space Odyssey

Directed By: Stanley Kubrick
Released: 1968
IMDB score: 8.3
Cast: Keir Dullea, Gary Lockwood and William Sylvester
Synopsis: When a mysterious artifact is uncovered on the Moon, a spacecraft manned by two humans and one supercomputer is sent to Jupiter to find its origins.

The Thing

Directed By: John Carpenter
Released: 1982
IMDB score: 8.2
Cast: Kurt Russel, Wilford Brimley and Keith David
Synopsis: A research team in Antarctica is hunted by a shape-shifting alien that assumes the appearance of its victims.

Arrival

Directed By: Denis Villeneuve
Released: 2016
IMDB score: 7.9
Cast: Amy Adams, Jeremy Renner and Forest Whitaker
Synopsis: Linguist Louise Banks leads a team of investigators when gigantic spaceships touch down around the world. As nations teeter on the verge of global war, Banks and her crew must find a way to communicate with the extraterrestrial visitors.
